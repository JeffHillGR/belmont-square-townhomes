Welcome to Belmont Square

Belmont Square Townhomes offers a rare opportunity to own a brand-new, thoughtfully designed home in Belmont, Michigan — part of the highly regarded Rockford School District. Each townhome is built with quality craftsmanship, modern finishes, and the space you need to truly make it your own — all with a relatively care-free lifestyle.

Best of all, exterior maintenance is completely taken care of — landscaping and snowplowing are included, so you can enjoy your home without the weekend chores. Located in the Rockford area with easy access to parks, shopping, dining, and downtown Grand Rapids, Belmont Square puts you right where you want to be.
